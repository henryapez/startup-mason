Welcome to Startup Mason

Supporting and educating entrepreneurs in and around GMU and the DC Metro.

Our Mission

StartUp Mason is a peer to peer entrepreneurship learning group dedicated to building entrepreneurs in the GMU community. Group members undertake entrepreneurial initiatives while participating in peer-to-peer feedback sessions, multiple mentor meetings, and completing an entrepreneurship and innovation curriculum based on lean startup, customer development, business model canvas, and other open-source entrepreneurial tools.

Learn

Meetings, Startup visits, internships

Our bi-weekly meetings are open to everyone, regardless of major or whether you are actively working on a startup idea. Come learn about the startup world and other entrepreneurs in the Mason community. Check out our job and intership board as well - for a real glimpse of what it means to be an entrepreneur or to work at a startup.

Experiment

Workshops, Challenges

If you are interested in testing out or developing an idea, we hold workshops throughout the semester.
